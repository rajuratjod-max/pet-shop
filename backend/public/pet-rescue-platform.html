How Our Rescue Process Works

From rescue to rehoming, we ensure every pet gets the care and love they deserve

1

Report & Response

Call our hotline or submit a report. Our team responds immediately to assess the situation and coordinate rescue efforts.

2

Safe Rescue

Our trained rescue team safely retrieves the animal using proper equipment and techniques to minimize stress and injury.

3

Medical Care

Immediate veterinary examination and treatment. We provide all necessary medical care, vaccinations, and rehabilitation.

4

Foster & Recovery

Animals stay in loving foster homes or our facility during recovery, receiving socialization and behavioral training.

5

Forever Home

Once healthy and ready, we match pets with perfect families through our comprehensive adoption process.
