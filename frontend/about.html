Our Story

Who We Are

PawsConnect was founded in 2020 with a simple but powerful mission: to ensure every animal finds a loving, permanent home. We are a dedicated team of animal lovers, veterinarians, and volunteers who work tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need.

Our Mission

To create lasting bonds between animals and families through compassionate care, education, and responsible adoption practices.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Every animal deserves love and care
  • Integrity: Honest and transparent in all our practices
  • Community: Building stronger bonds through pet adoption
  • Excellence: Providing the highest standard of animal care
